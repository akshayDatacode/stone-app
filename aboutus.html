About Us

Mr Avinash Gupta
(Our Founder)

Surya Stone was established in the year 1981 and is presently India's leading Natural stone export company in manufacturing and export of high quality natural stones. Our specialties are Sandstone, Limestone [natural, calibrated, honed, mirror polished], cobbles and pebbles. The company is among the largest producers in India and exports its product line around the World to the most demanding markets and clientele.

OUR PHILOSOPHY

The true assets of any organization are its people. We have a team of dedicated and skilled workers. Our organizational culture encourages the quest for excellence and a progressive and futuristic attitude.

Quality is of paramount importance to us and we are constantly elevating our standards of excellence for offering better quality products and services. We stand on the edifice of quality and innovation. We ensure that all our products are high in quality and durability. Our quality control engineers have been stationed at the quarries to maintain a strict vigil on the manufacturing process. All the products have inspection reports and test certificates to avoid any defects.

OUR INFRASTRUCTURE

Our infrastructure has played an important role in giving us the status that we have acquired in the market. We possess a capacious warehouse which has the capability to store large quantities of products. We also offer speedy and efficient transportation facilities which help us to deliver products within the stipulated time. Our sourcing experts source only the finest quality products from noted companies. We are Leading Natural stone export company in India.

WINGS OF ASPIRATIONS!!

In order to amplify our hegemonic role in the industry, we synchronize with our customers to positively improve the standards for quality and strive to make business deals feasible at a competitive market price. We believe that our future is anchored to our caliber to achieve equilibrium with customer expectations and our goal is to surpass the zone of expectations.

Surya Stone is into export of natural stone field and offer a wide variety of stones to suit present day market trends.
